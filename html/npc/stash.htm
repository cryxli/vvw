Private Stash

When you start the game Private Stash is not present in Castle Zagore. You have to rescue him from the Sepulcher of the Damned before he offers his services.

Speaking with him, it is possible to deposit and retrieve any kind of items. "He is" the shared stash among all your characters.

Private Stash is a coward. But that is most likely the reason why he is still alive. Out of combat he provides a much more valuable service to the hunters than when standing in their way... or cowering behind them.
