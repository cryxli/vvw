Vail The Immportal

Vail the Immortal is the boss of the Mausoleum of Bones.

The lair of Vail is full of all kind of undead, from the Skeleton, various Wraiths, and several Gargoyles. Vale is revived until his phylactery is destroyed, unlike the two Shard Sentinels and the phylactery itself, Vale has few health and not much defense. His attacks are Seeking Projectiles.
