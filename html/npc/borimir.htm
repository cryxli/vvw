Tzar Borimir

Tzar Borimir is the founder and first king of Zagoravia, first hunter and founder of the Hunters Order. Actually, he's a spirit, bound to Zagoravia by his ancient oath to protect the city and the world from demons.

He's an ancestor of the current Queen Katarina of Zagoravia.

As a quest giver he will direct Victor to the Well of Worlds in an attempt to lift the curse that has befallen the country.
