Queen Katarina

You meet her in Castle Zagore after the tutorial.

The main quest will reveal that Katarina and Victor have more in common than the first look may reveal. Both were tricked by Gabriel at one point in their lives.

She has no other purpose than acting as an occational quest giver.

Trivia

Note that Katarina is named "Queen" of Zagarovia while her ancestor is called Tzar Borimir. Where "Tzar" is an Emperor in English. This may inply that Zagarovia once was the heart of an empire and has since declined to a single kingdom.
