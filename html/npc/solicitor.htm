Wa Lee The Solicitor

The demon Wa-Lee, also known as the Solicitor, is the boss encountered in the Demon World.

The Solicitor is a demon that you could only pray to never encounter. A being of ancient power, he is the one who oversees the fulfilment of demonic contracts signed by various foolish mortals. He has a symbiotic connection to the binding force of those contracts - while he protects them, he can also channel the power they provide. This allows him to call upon them in time of need, making him a resourceful and crafty opponent.

He can use multiple demon powers: multiple Boomerangs, Meteor and Blink, besides summoning essence and spider spawners.
