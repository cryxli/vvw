GebaN the Broker

GebaN is a Reaper that becomes a merchant when defeated.

He sells legendary and rare weapons and Destiny Cards depending on the location where you find him.

Cemetery Grounds

Found in the northwestern corner of the map. He appears after killing the porter and the dancing skeletons.

Among other items he sells the Pumpkin Hammer.

The Merchant Quarter

He will appears after killing some voracious vampire lurker.

Among other items he sells the Chimera.

Bottomless Pit

He stands on the starting platform.

Among other items he sells the rapier Finger of Chaos.

Cauldron of Chaos

Comes with The Cauldron of Chaos DLC.

At the end of the cauldron, once you defeated all the daily challenges, he will trade Demon Souls for items

DLCs

In the Fractured Worlds DLC he is the only merchant in The Locus.

In the Motörhead DLC he is an auxillary vendor of rare weapons located in the bathrooms of The Pub at the End of Time. He does not sell any destiny cards, here.
