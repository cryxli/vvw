Apocalypse

Apocalypse is the boss encountered on the balcony of Castle Zagore, where Queen Katarina is usually found. As Tzar Borimir tells, Apocalypse was trapped inside the frozen cave of Heart of Frost and was released to attack Castle Zagore.

Strategy

He is possibly the most difficult boss in the game since you are not completely geared up when you have to fight him and his attacks are quite diverse.

Use synergies: Slide into him with a tome's Dimension Wave then immediately swap to your hammer to deal damage based on the hammer's stats. Hammers deal more base damage than tomes. Second best choice would be scythes.

Kill one of his summoned aspects at a time to immobilise him. This gives you more time to do your combo attack. Also, keep the aspects away from you, e.g., using Shockwave demon power.

You may have to stand in Apocalypse's damaging floor to deal damage to him. Therefore, prepare a Healing Potion or two. The Diamond demon power may add another layer or protection.
