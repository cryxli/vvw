Irene

Irene is a hunter like Victor. She arrived a year ago in Zagoravia answering Queen Katarina's call.

Irene soon understands that there's something suspicious about the demon's sudden arrival and their tendency to never wander too far from Zagoravia. Instead of directly fighting them them, she starts investigating in the city.

After Victor finds her in the Royal Gardens she returns to the castle and acts as an alternative trader to Damian.

Soon therafter she dissapears again. Victor finds her talking to the late Tzar Borimir in the Widow's Mine. After that she stays in the castle offering her services for good.
