Chromatic Essence

The Chromatic Essence looks like a single essence at the end of The Devil's Throat. When destroyed it will split into the obligatory three lesser essences, but it will also spawn three new "greater" essences of another type.

Strategy

It easily fills the whole area, making evasion incredibly hard.

Kill the first essence, then start using area of effect damage like Demon Powers, tome's arcane power, etc. Use Aether Vial to fill your overdrive meter, if necessary. Also, life stealing on your weapons may buy you some time.

The general rule holds; keep moving, don't let yourself getting cornered, keep attacking slowly but surely reducing the number of enemies.
