Talismans

Talismans were introduced with the Fractured Worlds DLC.

A talisman is a chargeable armor piece that gives cosmetic wings. Once charged its effect can be triggered.

Charging

Talismans always have a prefix that determines how charge is built.

Prefix Effect

Depending on your playstyle some of the triggers may never apply. However, since you constantly dealing damage to enemies, Brute is almost always viable.
