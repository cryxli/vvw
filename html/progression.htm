Character Progression

Victor Vran starts at level one with:

  • 1 weapon slot, with a common Sword
  • 1 demon power slot, with Meteor
  • 1 consumable item slot
  • 1 talisman slot (with Fractured Worlds DLC)
  • starting outfit (You start with Hunter's Outfit, later at Castle Zagore you can choose one from Hunter's Outfit, Cavalier's Outfit and Vigilante's Outfit)
  • 1000 Health Points
  • 4 Destiny Points

Starting from level 1, each level will take 1000 experience points plus 200 per level gained in order to get to the next level up until level 47. Beyond level 47, the experience required to progress is altered. Beyond level 50, the experience to the next level is always 20,000, including the Renown levels available once you have hit level 60.
